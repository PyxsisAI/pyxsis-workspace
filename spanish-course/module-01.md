# Module 1 — Sounds & Survival 🟢

**Goal:** Say the sounds correctly, greet anyone, be polite, and introduce yourself to a patient.

---

## Part 1 — The 5 vowels (this is the #1 thing)
Spanish vowels are PURE and always the same. Master these and your accent jumps instantly.

| Letter | Sound | Like in English | Example |
|--------|-------|-----------------|---------|
| A | "ah" | f**a**ther | casa (KAH-sah) = house |
| E | "eh" | b**e**d | café (kah-FEH) = coffee |
| I | "ee" | s**ee** | sí (see) = yes |
| O | "oh" | n**o** | no (noh) = no |
| U | "oo" | f**oo**d | tú (too) = you |

**Quick rules:**
- **H** is always silent → *hola* = "OH-lah"
- **J** sounds like a hard English H → *trabajo* = "trah-BAH-ho"
- **LL** sounds like "y" → *llama* = "YAH-mah"
- **Ñ** sounds like "ny" → *mañana* = "mah-NYAH-nah"
- **RR** is rolled. Don't stress about it yet.

## Part 2 — Greetings & Courtesy (Track A)

| Spanish | Pronunciation | English |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| Hola | OH-lah | Hi / Hello |
| Buenos días | BWEH-nohs DEE-ahs | Good morning |
| Buenas tardes | BWEH-nahs TAR-dehs | Good afternoon |
| Buenas noches | BWEH-nahs NOH-chehs | Good evening / night |
| ¿Cómo está? | KOH-moh es-TAH | How are you? (polite) |
| Bien, gracias | bee-EHN GRAH-see-ahs | Well, thank you |
| Por favor | por fah-VOR | Please |
| Gracias | GRAH-see-ahs | Thank you |
| De nada | deh NAH-dah | You're welcome |
| Sí / No | see / noh | Yes / No |
| Adiós | ah-dee-OHS | Goodbye |
| Lo siento | loh see-EHN-toh | I'm sorry |

> 💡 **Polite vs casual:** Use *usted* (polite "you") with patients and elders. We'll default to polite — it's the respectful form for your work.

## Part 4 — Medical Mini-Set (Track B)

| Spanish | Pronunciation | English |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| ¿Cómo se siente? | KOH-moh seh see-EHN-teh | How do you feel? |
| ¿Tiene dolor? | tee-EH-neh doh-LOR | Are you in pain? |
| Respire, por favor | res-PEE-reh | Breathe, please |
| Tranquilo / Tranquila | trahn-KEE-loh | Stay calm / relax |
| Todo está bien | TOH-doh es-TAH bee-EHN | Everything is okay |

---

## Part 5 — Mini-Dialogue (read it out loud)

> **Nurse:** Buenos días. Me llamo Bethany. Soy enfermera.
> **Patient:** Buenos días.
> **Nurse:** ¿Cómo se llama usted?
> **Patient:** Me llamo José.
> **Nurse:** Mucho gusto, José. ¿Cómo se siente hoy?
> **Patient:** No muy bien. Tengo dolor.
> **Nurse:** Lo siento. Estoy aquí para ayudarle. Tranquilo.
