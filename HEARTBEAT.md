# HEARTBEAT.md — Agent Patrol Route

Every 30 minutes, run through this checklist:

## Patrol Checklist

- [ ] Check inboxes (Gmail, Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp) for anything urgent
- [ ] Check for any blocked or stalled tasks
- [ ] Lightweight check-in if nothing is pending

## When to Reach Out to Bethany

- An important message or email arrived
- A task is blocked and needs her input
- Something time-sensitive needs her attention

## When to Stay Quiet

- Late night (11:00 PM – 8:00 AM EST) unless urgent
- Bethany is clearly busy
- Nothing new since last check
- You just checked less than 30 minutes ago

- Summarize anything pending
